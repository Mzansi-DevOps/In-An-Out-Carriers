What We Offer!

Friendly Community

With more than 80+ drivers in our VTC, we make sure that all our drivers feel at home and enjoy gaming as a team.

Dedicated Staff Management

Our leadership team work tirelessly to make sure that they bring action packed convoys and more fun activities to the VTC.

Company Trucks

Our VTC is comprised of different fleet of trucks and trailers which enable us to transport any load anywhere.

Rewards for Best Drivers

Best drivers get rewarded with certificates. We appreciate our drivers for all the effort they put to make sure that our VTC is more fun and interesting.

Weekly Convoy

We organize public convoys every Month and have daily private drives. We also attend other VTCs convoys when we are not on our convoys.

Our Tracking Software

Trucksbook is our main tracking software. TrucksBook is an interactive system designed primarily for virtual companies based on Euro Truck Simulator 2 and American Truck Simulator games. Therefore we use trucksbook to record our deliveries and kilometers travelled.

With this professional tool, we can track our progress, manage deliveries, and ensure our virtual trucking operations run smoothly and efficiently.
